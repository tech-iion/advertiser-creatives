Core Mechanics

How merging, spawning, and zone unlocking work together.

üß© The Merge Grid

A 5√ó5 grid sits at the center of gameplay. Players tap a "Spawn" button to generate Tier 1 raw ingredient items (bubbles, drops, petals, etc.) on an empty cell. Dragging matching items onto each other merges them up one tier. The grid is persistent items stay between sessions.

  • Two identical items merge into the next tier
  • Tier 5 items are final AllKinds products (Whipped Shower Foam, Jelly Wash, etc.)
  • Completed products can be "shelved" placed into an unlocked shop zone
  • Shelving a product clears the grid cell and awards coins + XP

üì¶ Spawning

Spawning costs coins (soft currency). Each spawn places a random Tier 1 item on an empty cell. If the grid is full, player must merge or sell items before spawning. Spawn cost increases slightly as the player progresses to create light resource tension.

  • Base cost: 10 coins
  • Cost scales +2 per zone unlocked
  • Special "Golden Spawn" (rare): drops a Tier 2 directly

üîì Zone Unlocking

The isometric shop has 7 zones, each themed to an AllKinds product category. Zones unlock when the player reaches a specific XP threshold. Once unlocked, the zone appears in the shop view and can be stocked with matching Tier 5 products.

  • Zone 1 (Bath & Body): Unlocked by default
  • Zones 2‚Äì7: Unlock at XP milestones
  • Each zone has 4‚Äì6 product slots to fill

üéØ The "Shelving" Action

When a Tier 5 product is created, it glows on the grid. The player taps it to see a "Shelve in Shop" button. This transitions to the isometric shop view where the product flies to its zone shelf with a satisfying animation. This is the core reward moment watching your shop come to life product by product.

Merge Chain System

The 5-tier merge chain from raw ingredients to finished AllKinds products.

üîó Universal Merge Chain

Every product follows a 5-tier chain. Each tier has a distinct visual identity that evolves from abstract ingredients to recognizable AllKinds packaging. The chain is thematic per product category Bath & Body items might start as water droplets, while Skincare starts as botanical leaves.

‚ú® Category-Specific Ingredients

To keep merging visually engaging, each product category spawns its own themed Tier 1 items. Bath & Body spawns bubbles and water drops. Skincare spawns botanical leaves. Hair Care spawns sparkle wisps. Makeup spawns color pigment dots. Fro-Yo spawns fruit bits. This means the grid is always colorful and varied.

üé≤ Merge Output Logic

When merging Tier 4 ‚Üí Tier 5, the specific product created is determined by the ingredient category. Each category has a pool of possible products. The first time a product is created, it's celebrated with a "NEW!" animation. Duplicate products can be sold for bonus coins or gifted to NPC customers for XP.

  • First creation of any product: 50 XP + 30 coins + "Discovery" badge
  • Duplicate products: 15 coins (sell) or 25 XP (gift to NPC)
  • Product catalog tracks all discovered items

  • Each product has a real description
  • "Tap to Shop" link to allkinds.com

Economy & Game Loop

Currency flow, retention hooks, and the core engagement loop.

üí∞ Currency: Coins (Soft Only)

Single currency keeps it simple for the target age group. No premium/hard currency this is a brand game, not a monetization play. Coins flow freely to keep the merge loop satisfying.

  • Earned by: shelving products (+30), selling duplicates (+15), completing objectives (varies), watching the shop generate income over time
  • Spent on: spawning items (10‚Äì22 coins), cosmetic shop decorations (optional), unlocking special merge recipes (optional)
  • Starting balance: 100 coins (enough for ~10 spawns)

Rounded, friendly sans-serif (matching AllKinds brand font). Headings bold and playful. Numbers and scores use a slightly bouncy display weight for fun factor.

üèóÔ∏è

Clean, colorful iso art style matching the storyboard. Characters are simple, stylized figures. Products on shelves use real AllKinds packaging rendered in iso perspective.

Merge = particle pop + haptic. Shelve = product flies to shop with trail. Zone unlock = confetti burst + chime. Grid item hover = gentle wobble. Everything feels tactile and responsive.

üè™ Isometric Shop Details (From Storyboard)

The shop follows the storyboard's L-shaped layout. Main entrance at bottom-center. Each zone is a distinct "room" with its own wall color matching the zone theme. Shelves, display tables, and product stands populate each room. NPCs (cute tween characters) browse the shop as it gets stocked more customers appear as the shop fills up, giving a sense of life and success.

Tech Notes

Implementation considerations for the Phaser.js build.

‚öôÔ∏è Engine & Framework

  • Phaser 3 primary game engine
  • Isometric plugin or custom iso renderer
  • Portrait mode, 9:16 aspect ratio
  • Target: Mobile web (iOS Safari + Chrome Android)
  • Asset loading: lazy per zone (reduce initial load)

üíæ Data & Persistence

  • LocalStorage for save data (grid state, coins, XP, unlocked zones)
  • Optional: Backend save if deployed as Telegram Mini App or embedded in AllKinds site
  • Product catalog stored as JSON config easy to update
  • Analytics events for zone unlocks, product discoveries, shop completions

üé® Asset Requirements

  • Merge Grid Items: 5 tiers √ó 7 categories = ~35 unique item sprites (plus color variants)
  • Shop Zones: 7 isometric room tiles with themed decorations
  • Product Sprites: ~34 real AllKinds products rendered in iso style
  • Characters: 5‚Äì8 NPC customer sprites (idle + walk animations)
  • UI: Buttons, panels, tabs, currency display, progress bars, particle effects
  • Audio: Merge SFX, coin SFX, unlock fanfare, ambient shop music (optional)

üìê Key Scenes (Phaser)

  • BootScene Preloader, splash
  • MergeScene 5√ó5 grid, spawn button, drag-and-drop logic, merge detection
  • ShopScene Isometric shop render, zone states, product shelving animation
  • CatalogScene Collection grid, product detail modal, shop links
  • UIOverlay Persistent HUD (coins, XP bar, zone progress, tab navigation)

üöÄ Deployment Options

Built as a standard HTML5 web game, it can deploy to: allkinds.com as an embedded experience, Telegram Mini App for social sharing, standalone PWA for app-store-like access, or kiosk mode for AllKinds retail stores (12 locations in Australia). The codebase stays the same only the wrapper and save backend changes per platform.
